About me & the Bookish Travels

I'm Larissa and I'm currently travelling the world with my partner. We started in Berlin and are planning to go to Eastern Europe, Asia and then New Zealand. P.S.: I will add all books in the language I read them in. If there is no English translations available, this will be noted below.
